Read Reciept

Email senders can use a trick called a tracking pixel to know exactly when you open their emails, just like a read reciept.

These pixels are not only hard to detect, but they also give away lots of personal information!

Using this tool, you can receive an email containing my implementation of a tracking pixel to see the type of information you're giving away simply by opening an email.
